Building a cultural archive online, together.

Sights and Sounds Digital Archive

The Sights & Sounds Digital Archive is a project created by students at the Georgia Institute of Technology and sponsored by Next Steps and the Sights and Sounds Black Cultural Museum.

Sights & Sounds

Sights & Sounds is a black cultural museum in Atlanta that houses over 8,000 artifacts ranging from Ancient Egypt to Michael Jackson to Barack Obama

Next Steps

Next Steps is a non-profit organization reaching out to local youth in Atlanta to revolutionize STEM education by advocating for leadership and entrepreneurship.
